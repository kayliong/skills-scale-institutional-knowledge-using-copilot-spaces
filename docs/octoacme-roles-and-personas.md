# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove blockers, and promote continuous improvement. They serve the team by ensuring adherence to agile principles and fostering a healthy, productive team environment.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and blockers for the development team
- Shield the team from external distractions and scope creep
- Coach the team on agile best practices and self-organization
- Track and report on sprint health metrics (velocity, burndown)

### Goals
- Enable high-performing, self-organizing teams
- Ensure smooth sprint execution and predictable delivery
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-ones with team members to address concerns
- Coordination with Project Manager and Product Manager on process alignment

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Works with **Project Manager** to align delivery timelines and manage risks
- Partners with **Product Manager** to ensure backlog readiness and sprint goals
- Supports **Developers** by removing technical and process blockers

---

## UX Designer / Researcher

### Role Summary
UX Designers/Researchers ensure user needs and usability are considered throughout the product lifecycle. They conduct research, design interfaces, and validate solutions to maximize product impact and user satisfaction.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research (interviews, surveys, usability testing)
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Product Manager to define user flows and requirements
- Validate designs through user feedback and A/B testing
- Maintain design systems and ensure UI consistency

### Goals
- Maximize product usability and user satisfaction
- Ensure design decisions are grounded in user research
- Reduce friction and improve conversion/engagement metrics

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research readouts and findings presentations
- Collaboration in sprint planning to scope design work

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Partners with **Product Manager** to translate user needs into features
- Collaborates with **Developers** to ensure designs are technically feasible
- Works with **Technical Writer** to ensure user-facing documentation is clear

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers own documentation of architectures, APIs, user guides, and process documentation. They ensure clarity and accessibility of technical content for internal teams and external users.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain API documentation, user guides, and tutorials
- Document system architectures and technical specifications
- Create and update process documentation and runbooks
- Review and edit technical content for clarity and accuracy
- Collaborate with engineering to capture knowledge and best practices

### Goals
- Ensure all technical and process documentation is accurate and up-to-date
- Improve onboarding experience for new team members and users
- Reduce support burden through clear self-service documentation

### Typical Communication
- Documentation review sessions with engineering
- Coordination with Product Manager on feature documentation needs
- Release notes collaboration with Release Manager

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Works with **Developers** to document technical implementations
- Partners with **Product Manager** to ensure user documentation aligns with features
- Collaborates with **Support/Customer Success Lead** to address common user questions

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers plan and coordinate releases, manage deployment checklists, and ensure clear communication of release status and readiness across stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule releases aligned with project milestones
- Create and manage release checklists and readiness criteria
- Coordinate with development, QA, and DevOps on deployment activities
- Communicate release status, risks, and timelines to stakeholders
- Document release notes and change logs

### Goals
- Ensure reliable, predictable releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment risk through proper planning and communication
- Maintain clear release documentation and audit trails

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings and go/no-go decisions
- Release status updates to stakeholders
- Post-release reviews and retrospectives

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Coordinates with **Project Manager** on release timelines and dependencies
- Works with **DevOps Engineer** on deployment automation and infrastructure
- Partners with **Technical Writer** on release documentation

---

## Support / Customer Success Lead

### Role Summary
Support/Customer Success Leads serve as the primary point of contact for user feedback, support escalations, and post-release education. They ensure users are successful and their feedback is incorporated into product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Manage user support requests and escalations
- Collect and synthesize user feedback for product improvements
- Coordinate incident communication with affected users
- Develop user education materials and onboarding resources
- Track customer satisfaction and success metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Ensure rapid resolution of user issues and escalations
- Channel user feedback into actionable product improvements

### Typical Communication
- Customer support channels (tickets, chat, email)
- Internal escalation meetings with Product and Engineering
- Customer success reviews and check-ins

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Works with **Product Manager** to prioritize user-reported issues
- Coordinates with **Project Manager** on incident response and communication
- Partners with **Technical Writer** to improve help documentation

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers enable automated pipelines, manage infrastructure, and monitor system health. They ensure reliable, repeatable deployments and rapid incident resolution.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment
- Manage infrastructure provisioning and configuration
- Monitor system health, performance, and security
- Respond to production incidents and support root cause analysis
- Implement infrastructure-as-code and automation best practices

### Goals
- Ensure high availability and reliability of production systems
- Reduce deployment time and risk through automation
- Enable rapid incident detection and resolution

### Typical Communication
- Collaboration in sprint planning on infrastructure work
- Incident response coordination and post-mortems
- Infrastructure and deployment documentation updates

### Cross-functional Interactions
- Works with **Developers** on build, test, and deployment automation
- Partners with **Release Manager** to coordinate deployment activities
- Supports **Project Manager** with infrastructure-related risk management
